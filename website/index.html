Why CTRL

Not yet another local AI client.

Three things make it different — and none of them is "we host a model for you".

⌘

Ambient, not an app you open

One global hotkey, one morphing conversation — no tab-soup. Capabilities live in an open registry (MCP / CLI / Skills), so the surface stays simple while the ecosystem scales.

⚿

Bring your own everything

Your keys, your models, your brain. No account, no markup, no default model spend. Keys live in your OS keychain — we literally can't read them.

⌯

Plain-text all the way down

Notes, tools, skills, memory — all markdown you can open in vim in 100 years. There's no "export", because nothing was ever imported.
